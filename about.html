BOCA Band of Consenting Adults

BOCA is a high-energy cover band based in Southside Hampton Roads, known for powerhouse vocals, ear-grabbing solos, and a setlist that spans decades and genres. Whether it’s a dance-floor classic, a throwback anthem, or a modern hit, BOCA brings every song to life with their own unique spin. BOCA is more than just a band — we’re all about connection. With each other, with the music, and most of all, with the crowd. Every show is crafted to get people moving, singing, and feeling like part of something electric. At the end of the day, we’re a family of musicians who love what we do — and we’re here to make sure you’re having just as much fun as we are.
